Our Profile
Brophy Family and Youth Services is the primary provider for youth and family services in South West Victoria. Brophy originated in 1974 with its beginnings in hostel accommodation for homeless youth. Over the years, it has developed a comprehensive range of services for young people, individuals and families. The agency provides regional services to Portland, Hamilton, Camperdown, Warrnambool and districts through its outreach programs. Its main office is located in Warrnambool and sub regional offices in Hamilton and Portland.

Our Vision
People are meaningfully connected to create the life they want. We achieve this together through innovation and relationships, creating new horizons towards an advantaged community.

Our Mission
Our mission is to provide community based services that promote a just society and improve the life circumstances for people who are vulnerable and disadvantaged, especially those who are experiencing homelessness, family violence, marginalisation or disconnectedness.

Our Values
Brophy Family and Youth Services actively values and promotes diversity in our community and affirms our commitment to always be inclusive and respectful to all, regardless of gender, age, race, sex, sexual orientation, religion, level of ability, cultural or language background.

We welcome people who are LGBTIQ+ (lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, gender diverse, intersex, queer, plus other sexual minority identities).


Our values are
Social Justice – promoting justice, social fairness and human rights.
Professionalism – practising in an ethical, respectful and inclusive manner.
Empowerment – strengthening and enabling individual and community decision-making.
Responsiveness – responding in a timely, engaging and respectful manner.
Partnership – striving for shared connections to create better client outcomes.


Brophy Family and Youth Services is committed to a culture of continuous quality improvement which ensures quality services are delivered to our clients and stakeholders.
We are accredited through Quality Innovation Performance on a 3 year accreditation cycle under the following sets of standards:
Australian General Practice Accreditation Limited (AGPAL) - Full accreditation in July 2017
Department of Health and Human Services: Human Services Standards - Full Accreditation in March 2018.
Quality Improvement Council: Health and Community Services Standards - Full Accreditation in March 2018, achieving 2 exceeds in the areas of: Collaboration and Strategic Positioning and Community and Professional Capacity Building.
Gay & Lesbian Health Victoria Standards: Rainbow Tick Standards - Full Accreditation in March 2018, we are now one of 25 agencies and services across Australia to be accredited under these standards.
National Standards for Mental Health Services – Full accreditation in March 2018
At the 2018 accreditation the surveyors noted:
“ Brophy are known in the sector as willing collaborators with a genuine commitment to improve outcomes and develop services in the region to improve outcomes for young people and families".
“ Client feedback highlights the achievements made by clients through having choices and opportunities opened up to them through their links with Brophy. If not for Brophy young people noted that "they do not know where they would currently be".


Brophy Family and Youth Services are seeking enthusiastic and talented people who are passionate about helping us build an advantaged community where people are meaningfully connected to create the life they want.
If you have qualifications in social or community services work, a passion for empowering others to make positive choices and decisions about their lives, and experience in providing professional, high quality case management, advocacy and support to clients - then we want to hear from you!
We pride ourselves on being a great place to work. You care for our clients and we care for you, including investing in your development and wellbeing. We offer a variety of training and development options, above award wages, salary packaging benefits and flexible working arrangements including wellbeing days.
